My Top 3 Favorite Movies Of All Time

THE BLACKLIST

The movie is breathtaking with very intense plot twists that keeps you on the edge wondering what comes next. I consider it my favorite movie of all time.

BREAKING BAD

An absolute brilliant masterpiece!! Breaking Bad is as good as everyone says it is. What sets it apart from most of the shows around is how close it is to reality.

GAME OF THRONES

This is very likely the best TV show ever made in terms of suspense, character development, plot, effects, acting.....10/10 with no doubt.
